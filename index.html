The open source solution for your community.

Say goodbye to the hassle of relying on insecure sources like Bopimo or Vertineer , they're unmaintained and Leaks .

Castilla is the open source solution for your sandbox community needs. With builtin state-of-the-art security and an easy set up, we're here for you.

What databases does Castilla support?

Castilla supports 3 Database paragidms, NoSQL, SQL an Flat . Which in popular names are: MongoDB & Similar, MysQL / MariaDB, Postgre and SQLITE

What happens if I don't respect the license?

Unlike Bopimo and Vertinner leaks (which are unlicensed), open source software is protected behind a license that acts as a shield. GitHub, Digital Ocean, Cloudflare and many other services most developers use make sure that their clients respect the license of the software hosted too. Specially open source one. If someone is found not respecting the license, it takes 10 minutes to contact and take down a website until it is liable to the conditions listed before. This is not a threat, just a warning since as mentioned above, it's a lot of work to maintain this project...

Do I need to open source my site to use Castilla?

No need! Castilla is made so you can keep it as private as you want. But always keeping in mind the conditions listed in the license.

Why the name?

Castilla is a province in Spain, I find myself living close to it, and like it's name. Thus the project name!

Why are you doing this project?

I'm tired of seeing Bopimo websites getting their data leaked and websites down, It's sad and dangerous for the average user. Who might not be able of seeing how bad the situation is. Since Bopimo is unmaintained, and It's former developers (Firecatmagic etc...) encourage people not to use it, there is no real good Open Source and Maintained Solution .

There are some open source websites but they are completely unmaintained, or the developers don't hold good reputation amongst the community.
